Paloma

Description

Delicious Summer Cocktail: The Paloma

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. tequila
  • 1/2 lime, plus a wheel for garnish
  • Grapefruit soda
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Pour salt into a small plate. Rim a Collins glass with lime then roll in salt, turning to coat. Top with grapefruit soda and stir slightly with a bar spoon.
  2. Fill glass with ice. Add tequila, juice of the lime, and a pinch of salt into the glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel before serving.
